Decision Systems Group

Decision Systems Group, started on November 2011. 

The group is led by Prof. Stavros A.Karkanis form the Department of Informatics and Computer Technology.

The aim of the Group is the development of simple technological & scientific projects along with students from the Departments of TEI.

A number of students from the Electronics and Informatics Departments of the Institute has been assigned to the DS Group having each one of them different responsibilities in the developments.

Two are the main projects developed till today : 

a) Autonoumous Vehicle V1-TEILAM.
b) GSM - Plug

The members of our Group are enthousiastic with their developments and we all seek to expand our cooperations for future technological projectss.
